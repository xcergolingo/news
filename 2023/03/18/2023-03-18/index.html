About: AI

Insights:

  • A critique of AI has been made, claiming that it gets everything wrong.
  • There have been no major breakthroughs in the academic discipline of AI for a couple of decades.
  • AI models take human culture and digest it. But human culture will, in turn, digest these models – adjusting their role, connotations, significance… Content-generation models only exist to the extent that their content gets consumed by humans and integrated into human culture.

About: LLM (Large Language Models)

Insights:

  • Theoretically, by routing all communications and tasks through an office assistant LLM, a 10-year old should be able to covertly pretend to be an adult professional – apply for remote jobs, actually hold one for a little while, etc…
  • Automatic Reasoning & Tool-Use of LLMs retrieves examples of reasoning & tool use from task library, writes its own program, pauses when tool call is encountered, integrates output. It is much better than few-shot prompting.
  • ART (Automatic multi-step reasoning and tool-use for large language models) achieves a substantial improvement over few-shot prompting and automatic CoT on unseen tasks in the BigBench and MMLU benchmarks.
  • Pydantic OutputParser can be used to define a schema in pure, canonical Python, add arbitrarily complex validators, and enforce type hints at run time for LLM output.

About: NeRF (Neural Radiance Fields)

Insights:

  • LERF (Language Embedded Radiance Fields) allows flexible natural language queries in 3D by grounding CLIP vectors volumetrically inside a NeRF.
  • NeRFMeshing distills Neural Radiance Fields into geometrically-accurate 3D meshes.

About: PhotoRoom

Insights:

  • PhotoRoom is a French startup that has been using deep learning for years to help vendors make product photos.
  • They have now developed a fast generative model to produce nice backgrounds on demand without requiring text prompts.

About: SemDeDup

Insights:

  • SemDeDup is a data-efficient learning at web-scale through semantic deduplication.
  • SemDeDup can remove 50% of the LAION dataset with minimal performance loss, effectively halving training time.

About: Unified Multi-Modal Latent Diffusion

Insights:

  • Unified Multi-Modal Latent Diffusion is used for joint subject and text conditional image generation.
