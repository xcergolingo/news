About: Brain-AI group at FAIR-Paris
Insights:

  • ConvNets are a decent model of how the ventral pathway of the human visual cortex works. But LLMs don’t seem to be a good model of how humans process language. There longer-term prediction taking place in the brain.
  • High-resolution image reconstruction with latent diffusion models from human brain activity.
  • Stable Diffusion Can Reconstruct Images From Brains. Give model human brain activity data (fMRI signals) and reconstructs high-resolution images w/ high fidelity. Studies how model components relate to different brain functions.

About: Dropout
Insights:

  • Dropout Reduces Underfitting. Finds that models equipped with early dropout achieve lower final training loss compared to their counterparts without dropout.
  • Propose early dropout and late dropout. Early dropout helps underfitting models fit the data better and achieve lower training loss. Late dropout helps improve the generalization performance of overfitting models.
