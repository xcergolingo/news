news 2023/02/26

About: LLaMA, Insights: LLaMA is a new open-source, high-performance large language model from Meta AI - FAIR. It is released under a GPL v3 license. It is a collection of foundation LLMs from 7B to 65B parameters. It has been trained on trillions of tokens from publicly available datasets. LLaMA-13B outperforms GPT-3 (175B) on most benchmarks and LLaMA-65B is competitive with the best models, Chinchilla70B and PaLM-540B.
About: T2I-Adapter, Insights: T2I-Adapter demo now supports multi-adapters. A demo is available.
About: LLMs, Insights: LLMs can be used as engines of text evolution. They can be used to generate document boilerplate, trace the provenance and assess the reliability of what people see and hear, and generate stable diffusion prompts, equations matching data, text sentiment, and Python code.
About: Prompt Injection Threats, Insights: Prompt Injection Threats to LLMs Using Tools is a paper that systematically analyzes threats facing app-integrated LLMs. It uses computer security principles to assess vulnerability and demonstrates the feasibility of attacks with synthetic apps.
About: Productivity, Insights: Productivity began to plateau right after the current wave of AI started getting deployed. For all the talk about ML destroying jobs, productivity had been improving fast long before we used any ML, and over the past 10 years we went from “very little ML” to to “ML deployed everywhere” and productivity stayed stagnant.
About: UX Improvement, Insights: One of the most valuable applications of LLMs is to generate document boilerplate for users, which is a UX improvement as many human systems have user-hostile interfaces.
