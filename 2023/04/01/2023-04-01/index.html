2023-04-01 AI news

news 2023/04/01

About: BloombergGPT
Insights: BloombergGPT is a 50 billion parameter language model that is trained on a wide range of financial data. It has constructed a 363 billion token dataset based on Bloomberg’s extensive data sources, perhaps the largest.

About: ChatGPT/HuggingGPT
Insights: HuggingGPT presents a system that leverages LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT) to connect various AI models in machine learning communities (e.g., HuggingFace) to solve AI tasks. It covers many sophisticated tasks across modalities and domains. Human requests something and ChatGPT plans tasks, selects AI models based on HuggingFace descriptions, manages cooperation of expert models to execute subtasks, and summarizes results.

About: CodeGeeX
Insights: CodeGeeX is a 13B multilingual model trained on 850 billion tokens of 23 programming languages.

About: DiffCollage
Insights: DiffCollage is a parallel generation of large content with diffusion models.

About: HyperDiffusion
Insights: HyperDiffusion generates implicit neural fields with weight-space diffusion.

About: Language Models
Insights: Language models can solve computer tasks by letting LLM to recursively criticize and improve its output, significantly outperforming existing LLM methods on computer tasks and surpassing supervised learning (SL) and RL approaches.

About: NeILF++
Insights: NeILF++ is inter-reflectable light fields for geometry and material estimation.

About: Token Merging
Insights: Token Merging for Fast Stable Diffusion uses nothing but pure python and pytorch, ToMe for SD speeds up diffusion by merging redundant tokens.

About: Word-As-Image
Insights: Word-As-Image for Semantic Typography is a Gradio demo that is out on HuggingFace Spaces.

About: AvatarCraft
Insights: AvatarCraft transforms text into neural human avatars with parameterized shape and pose control.

About: Forget-Me-Not
Insights: Forget-Me-Not is learning to forget in text-to-image diffusion models.
