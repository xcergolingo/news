news 2023/04/05

About: AI Alignment

Insights:

  • AI alignment will be manageable and less difficult than many have claimed.
  • Until we have a design for Human-Level AI, it’s mere speculation.
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) need therapists too.
  • SafeguardGPT attempts to apply “psychotherapy” on LLMs.
  • 4 types of agents: Therapist, Chatbot, Critic, User.
  • Human moderator simulates Chat/Therapy/Eval/Control phases to try to align to preferences.

About: ChatGPT

Insights:

  • Baize proposes a pipeline that can automatically generate a high-quality multi-turn chat corpus by leveraging ChatGPT to engage in a conversation with itself.
  • LLaMa-13B is an assistant-style large language model with ~800k GPT-3.5-Turbo Generations based on LLaMa.

About: CLIP model

Insights:

  • Kandinsky 2.1 inherits best practices from Dall-E 2 and Latent diffusion, while introducing some new ideas.
  • As text and image encoder, it uses CLIP model and diffusion image prior (mapping) between latent spaces of CLIP modalities. This approach increases the visual performance.

About: Language Models

Insights:

  • LLMMaps is a visual metaphor for stratified evaluation of large language models.
  • Stratified eval can reveal subfields where hallucinations are more likely to occur.
  • LLMMaps: new visualization transforms Q&A data + LLM responses into internal knowledge structures.
  • Compares BLOOM, GPT-3, ChatGPT, LLaMa-13B, etc.
  • Vision Transformers with Mixed-Resolution Tokenization.

About: LangChainAI

About: Rogue AI

Insights:

  • Julian argues that the likelihood of existential threats from rogue AI is insanely overstated.
  • Intelligence does not immediately cause an entity to want to “take over.”
  • A very dumb but specialized entity can kill a smarter one, e.g., virus vs. human.
  • MS Excel can do intelligent tasks and, in some ways, has already “taken over” our lives.
