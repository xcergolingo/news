2023-04-20 AI news

news 2023/04/20

About: AutoTaskFormer
Insights: AutoTaskFormer is a vision transformer that is designed for multi-task learning. It is capable of searching for the best transformer architecture for a given set of tasks. The paper and abstract are available.

About: Language Models
Insights: An evaluation of large language model outputs found that 80% of the outputs contained memorized data. However, the outputs containing the most memorized content were also more likely to be considered of high quality. A new sampling method called UniMax has been proposed to deliver more uniform coverage of head languages while mitigating overfitting on tail languages. The improved mC4 dataset and open source pretrained uMT5 models trained on 1T tokens are available. A new LLM called StableLM has been released with initial sets of 3B and 7B parameters, with 15B and 30B models on the way. The base models are released under CC BY-SA-4.0. A new LLM that can incrementally formalize word problems into equations and an external symbolic solver for equations have been combined to outperform PAL by 20% on a new dataset of challenging textbook Algebra word problems. NaturalSpeech 2 is a latent diffusion model that is capable of zero-shot speech and singing synthesis. The paper, project page, and abstract are available.

About: Latent Diffusion Models
Insights: Align your Latents is a high-resolution video synthesis with latent diffusion models that turns LDM Stable Diffusion into an efficient and expressive text-to-video model with resolution up to 1280 x 2048. Generative Disco is a text-to-video generation for music visualization. Text2Performer is a text-driven human video generation. Avatars Grow Legs is capable of generating smooth human motion from sparse tracking inputs with diffusion model. The papers, project pages, and abstracts are available.

About: Autonomous Agents
Insights: Four agent-related projects (AutoGPT, BabyAGI, CAMEL, and Generative Agents) have exploded recently. A blog has been written on how they differ from previous agents and how some key ideas have been incorporated. The link to the blog is available.

About: SAM-Adapter
Insights: SAM-Adapter is an adaptation of SAM in underperformed scenes such as camouflage, shadow, and more. The paper, project page, and abstract are available.

About: GPU Cloud Services
Insights: If Nvidia offered GPU cloud services, they would put themselves in competition with their biggest customers. Unless they plan to kill AWS and Azure, that would be a big mistake. The link to the tweet is available.
