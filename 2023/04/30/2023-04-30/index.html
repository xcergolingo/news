About: GPT-3.5

About: LangChainAI
Insights: LangChainAI has hosted a webinar on prompt injection with an all-star panel of guests including Simonw, Willpienaar, Kojinoshiba, Jonathan Cohen, and Christopher Parisien from Nvidia. Nvidia has recently released Guardrails, an open-source toolkit for controlling the output of a large language model.

About: LLM Agents
Insights: LLM agents in group settings with humans and AI have been developed, building new data of group conversations and showing state-of-the-art consistency, engagingness, identity, sensibility. Pairwise-trained LLMs lack the ability to decide when to talk and be coherent grounded on multiple characters.
