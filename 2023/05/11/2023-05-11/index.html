2023-05-11 AI news

news 2023/05/11

About: Diffusion AI
Insights:

  • Stable Diffusion AI Deepfake can de-age Harrison Ford. It uses SD+ControlNet+EbSynth+Fusion.
  • SUR-adapter enhances text-to-image pre-trained diffusion models with large language models.

About: FrugalGPT
Insights:

  • FrugalGPT is a way to use large language models while reducing cost and improving performance. It matches the performance of the best individual LLM (GPT-4) with 98% cost reduction or improves accuracy by 4% with the same cost.
  • FrugalGPT uses LLM cascade to learn which combinations of LLMs to use for different queries to reduce cost/improve accuracy.

About: Google
Insights:

  • Google announces PaLM 2, with improved multilingual, reasoning, and coding capabilities. It has been more heavily trained on multilingual text across over 100 languages.
  • Google Photos will gain a new ‘Magic Editor’ feature powered by generative AI.

About: Large Language Models
Insights:

  • Large Language Model Programs is the emerging methodology of embedding LLMs in a classic program to carry out more complex tasks.
  • Federated Instruction Tuning of LLMs leverages users’ diverse instructions stored on local devices while preserving privacy and data security. It is better than centralized training that has only limited local instructions.

About: Meta-FAIR
Insights:

  • IMAGEBIND is an open-source project by Meta-FAIR. It learns a joint embedding across six different modalities - images, text, audio, depth, thermal, and IMU data.

About: NeRFs
Insights:

  • NerfAcc proposes a toolbox that integrates advanced efficient sampling techniques that lead to significant speedups in training various recent NeRF papers with minimal modifications to existing codebases.

About: Personalized Robot Assistance
Insights:

  • TidyBot is a personalized robot assistance with large language models. The approach enables fast adaptation and achieves 91.2% accuracy on unseen objects in the benchmark dataset.

About: Plan-and-Execute Agents
Insights:

  • Plan-and-Execute Agents are a new type of agent inspired by BabyAGI and the recent Plan-and-Solve paper. They are better for more complex tasks, at the cost of more calls to the LLM.

About: Socratic Dialogue Among LLMs
Insights:

  • GPT agents autonomously develop problem-solving strategies in collaboration and execute their plans step-by-step without requiring further human intervention. They find solutions that elude single LLM-based agents.

About: Transformers
Insights:

  • Transformers Agents is a fully multimodal agent that can control 100,000+ HF models by talking to Transformers and Diffusers. It removes the barrier of entry to machine learning.
