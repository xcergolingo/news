About: AI pioneer Geoffrey Hinton
Insights: Geoffrey Hinton did not say that AI could wipe out humans. The journalist who wrote the CBC headline made that statement.

About: LLMs (Language Model Models)
Insights: Open source LLMs are becoming more common. MPT-7B is a new standard for open-source, commercially usable LLMs. It uses a context length of 65k tokens and is a transformer trained from scratch on 1T tokens of text and code. LLMs are good teachers of AI agents and can help humans reframe negative thoughts with more hopeful thoughts. Self-alignment of LLMs with minimal human labels is possible.
