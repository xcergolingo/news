news 2023/05/05

About: AI systems

Insights:

  • AI systems can be designed to be both super-intelligent and submissive to humans. (Geoffrey Hinton)
  • Superhuman AI systems will eventually exist, but we don’t have a basic design for them yet. (Geoffrey Hinton)
  • We can design AI to have superhuman intelligence and be submissive. For an entity to control another, it has to want to take control. (Geoffrey Hinton)
  • Distilling step-by-step can reduce both the model size and the amount of data required to outperform larger language models. (Multiple tweets)
  • DynamicStereo is a method for consistent dynamic depth from stereo videos. (Tweet)
  • AG3D is a method for learning to generate 3D avatars from 2D image collections. (Tweet)
  • Real-Time Radiance Fields is a method for single-image portrait view synthesis. (Tweet)
  • Visual Chain of Thought is a novel method that leverages chain of thought prompting with vision-language grounding to recursively bridge the logical gaps within sequential data. (Multiple tweets)
  • Multimodal Procedural Planning is a method for multimodal procedural planning via dual text-image prompting. (Tweet)

About: Intelligence

Insights:

  • There are very few examples of a more intelligent thing being controlled by a less intelligent thing. (Geoffrey Hinton)
  • Good political, business, and academic leaders surround themselves with staff whose members are often smarter than themselves. That’s a common counter-example of Geoffrey Hinton’s claim that more intelligent things almost always control less intelligent things. (Tweet)

About: Language models

Insights:

  • CodeGen2 is a method for training larger language models on programming and natural languages. (Multiple tweets)
  • Distilling step-by-step can extract language model rationales as supervision for small models. Compared to finetuning and distillation, it performs better with much less data. (Tweet)
  • The Flan-T5 model holds its ground well and even outperforms other language models like Dolly or StableLM. (Tweet)

About: Neuroscience

Insights:

  • Our neocortex is entirely controlled by our basal ganglia. Our future superhuman exocortex will be controlled by our neocortex. (Geoffrey Hinton)
  • Note to picky neuroscientists: replace “lizard brain” by “basal ganglia”. (Tweet)
