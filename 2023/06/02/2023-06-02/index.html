About: Language augmented CLIP (LaCLIP)
Insights:

  • LaCLIP is a simple yet highly effective approach to enhance CLIP training through language rewrites.
  • It leverages the in-context learning capability of large language models to rewrite the text.

About: Control4D
Insights:

  • It is built upon an efficient 4D representation with a 2D diffusion-based editor.
